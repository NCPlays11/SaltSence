How can I help the ocean outside of desalinization?

Even outside of the problem of desalinization there are still many things you can do to make Earth's oceans and seas a better place. As humans have industrialized, we create more and more trash that we can't get rid of. Trash is normally put into landfills, but sometimes trash escapes from the landfills and into bodies of water, polluting the Earth. This is why, you, can make a difference no matter where you are. These are just some of the things you can do to make marine life a better place.

Reduce Plastic

Conserve Water

Participate in Beach Cleanups

Each of these actions will reduce your impact on marine life. By reducing your plastic use you prevent a chance of that trash traveling to the ocean. Conserving water reduces the amount of water being desalinated from the ocean. And participating in beach cleanups helps you clean trash that has already made its way to the ocean.
