Why We Care

Desalination may provide fresh water, but its consequences are often overlooked. At SaltSense, we believe in raising awareness about the hidden dangers of desalination, including the damage to marine life, increased salinity in water bodies, and the release of harmful chemicals into the environment.

Discover More

Our Mission

Our mission is to educate communities, influence policy, and promote sustainable water solutions that protect our oceans and coastal ecosystems. We strive to foster a deeper understanding of the risks associated with desalination and advocate for innovative alternatives.

Marine Life We Care About

Sea Turtle

Sea turtles are threatened by pollution and habitat changes caused by desalination.

Coral Reef

Coral reefs are highly sensitive to increased salinity and pollutants from desalination.

Schooling Fish

Schooling fish are essential for the marine food web, affected by habitat disruptions.
